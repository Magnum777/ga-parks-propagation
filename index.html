About AE4XO

I'm a ham radio operator based in Warner Robins, Georgia, with a simple goal: activate every POTA-eligible park in the state. Four years ago, I set out with a portable rig and a tank of gas. In 2026, I finished the job.

Along the way, I've operated from coastal marshes at sunrise, battled humidity in pine forests, and learned more about Georgia's hidden gems than I ever expected. Every park has a story - and I'm here to tell them.

My partner in crime (and life) is KQ4GEC. We travel together and operate together, documenting our journey across Georgia's parks.
