About the Operator

Georgia Native.
Amateur Radio Nut.

Welcome to GA Parks and Propagation — your gateway to Georgia amateur radio adventure. We're your guides to both the wilderness and the airwaves, delving into the intricacies of amateur radio with tips, tricks, and tutorials to help you master the art of communication.

Whether you're a seasoned operator or just getting started, we journey through the great outdoors and the airwaves — one park and one contact at a time. All data on this site is pulled in real-time from official sources: POTA for activation logs and spots, NOAA for space weather, and YouTube for field reports.
