The Setup

What's in the go-bag after 500+ activations. Tested in Georgia heat, humidity, and the occasional sudden thunderstorm.

📻

ICOM IC-705

Primary portable rig. HF/VHF/UHF all-mode transceiver with built-in GPS and Bluetooth. Runs 5-10W on battery, perfect for field activations.

Transceiver
🎯

Chameleon MPAS 2.0

Multi-band portable vertical. Sets up in under 5 minutes, covers 160m-6m. Modular design adapts for any terrain - from sandy beaches to rocky ridges.

Antenna
⚡

Bioenno 12Ah LiFePO4

Field power that doesn't quit. 12Ah gives me 4-6 hours of operation at 50% duty cycle. Charges via solar panel between activations.

Power
🎒

REI Trail 40 Pack

Everything fits in one pack: radio, battery, antenna, coax, logging tablet, first aid, and snacks. With room to spare for a foldable chair.

Pack
📱

Digital Logger

iPad-based logging with auto-spotting to POTA. Syncs to the cloud, exports ADIF.

Logging
🔋

Goal Zero Nomad 20

20W solar panel for recharging between activations. Essential for multi-park days when you can't get back to the truck.

Solar
